Detailed Description

PureStorageDescription (ie Pure-0123456-CT0.FC0) breaks out the serial number and port information from the WWPN.

Although Pure re-purposes Qlogic HBAs, they have the generosity and forethought to rebadge the WWPN with their own OUI. Thanks, Pure!
