\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Cisco\+U\+C\+S\+Server\+Description}{p.}{classorg_1_1smallfoot_1_1wwn_1_1CiscoUCSServerDescription} (ie Cisco\+U\+C\+S-\/012-\/123\+:0) breaks out the uniqueness number and port index from the W\+W\+P\+N of a U\+C\+S-\/served physical host. 

I do tend to see 20000025b5hhhfnnn such that\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item hhh is a three-\/digit host index 
\item f is either 0 or 1 based on fabric A or B 
\item nnn is a three-\/digit server index 
\end{DoxyItemize}

The thing to remember about U\+C\+S is that the W\+W\+P\+N are entirely user-\/defined, are are U\+U\+I\+Ds, so this is merely a convention. It might fall apart on the next user!
